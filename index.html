How it works

  1. Work hard and gain valuable experience on the job.
  2. Add each and every new experience and skill to Resume Buddy. Don't limit yourself to your top few experiences.
  3. Use our AI enhancement tool to "Professionalize" your experience.
  4. When it comes time to apply for your next position, paste in the job description and let Resume Buddy pick your experiences that best fit the description.
